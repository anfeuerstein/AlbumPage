Homesick - A Day To Remember

Homesick

A Day To Remember

February 2, 2009

A Day To Remember's third album brings about an amazing blend of dense Metalcore with the more bouncy spring-loaded pop-punk sounds. This album really shows off frontman Jeremy McKinnon's vocal range from heavy metal laden tracks such as "NJ Legion Iced Tea" to softer more pop-punk inspired songs such as "If it Means A Lot To You."


Band Members

  • Jeremy McKinnon - Lead Vocals
  • Neil Westfall - Rhythm Guitar, Backing Vocals
  • Joshua Woodard - Bass Guitar
  • Alex Shelnutt - Drums
  • Kevin Skaff - Lead Guitar, Backing Vocals
